Read Before Registration 🚨

  • Attendance & Punctuality:

    Attend all sessions on time. Reschedule 24hr before. Teaching live classes 3x a week.

  • Cancellations:

    Less than 24hr notice maybe charged. Tutor cancellations get makeup sessions free.

  • Payments:

    Free One-week trial for new students. Pay on/before first session. Monthly 700-1100Br, Weekly 350-500Br Via Telebirr. Send screenshot to tutor.

  • Student Conduct:

    Respectful, collaborative, academic honesty enforced.

  • Session Preparation:

    Bring notes, textbooks, stable internet, quiet space.

  • Policies & Communication:

    Progress reports available. All info is confidential. Live sessions are given through Google Meet, Zoom or Whatsapp. Parents encouraged for younger students.

  • Acknowledgement:

    There will be student of the month and Hall of Fame. Students will be awarded digital certificates based on their performances and subjects they enrolled in by tutors. A student who lasts a year will recieve a special certificate!
